# rigyn identity

The Open Kernel Loom is rigyn's primary mark. Its vertical spine is the stable runtime and append-only session line. The interleaved center is the normalized event flow, the open side sockets are replaceable tools, providers, extensions, and hosts, and the filled diamond is the active session leaf. Every edge remains open so the mark reads as a programmable harness rather than a closed appliance.

Use the emblem by itself for terminal chrome, avatars, and compact spaces. Use the lowercase `rigyn` lockup once in a document or launch surface; do not repeat a large initial letter as decoration. Background treatments may extend cropped fragments of the same terminal-grid geometry, but foreground content must always win.

Primary colors are near-black `#090a0a` and warm terminal white `#f3f1e8`. Muted background geometry may use `#c8c5ba`. The primary mark does not use gradients, shadows, or accent colors.

Files:

- `rigyn-mark.svg` — canonical transparent vector mark.
- `rigyn-lockup.svg` — canonical mark with lowercase wordmark.
- `rigyn-avatar.svg` and `rigyn-avatar.png` — text-free square avatar.
- `rigyn-social.svg` and `rigyn-social.png` — 1280×640 repository preview without a tagline.
