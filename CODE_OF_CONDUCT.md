# Community conduct

rigyn discussions and contributions should be safe, practical, and focused on the work.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate with patience and respect, including during disagreement;
- critique ideas and evidence rather than a person's identity, experience, or motives;
- avoid harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory language, and deliberate disruption;
- protect private information and credentials;
- accept a maintainer's request to pause, move, or narrow a discussion;
- correct mistakes directly and make room for people with different backgrounds and communication styles.

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, project chat, and project-related private communication.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainer contact or private security-reporting channel. Include relevant links or records and the outcome needed for safety. Maintainers may remove content, limit participation, lock discussions, or ban a participant. They should minimize disclosure, avoid conflicts of interest, and explain an enforcement decision to the affected parties when it is safe to do so.
